Dr. Jason Wang

Dr. Jason Wang is a provincially licensed Doctor of Traditional Chinese Medicine and Acupuncturist who is also a long-time resident of Surrey for the last 20 years, serving patients in this growing city with dedication and care.



Treatments

Over the last 10 years I've been seeing patients with 50% orthopedic issues and 50% organ related problems(eg. digestive, nervous, etc). Herbal medicine works very well for the organ related group. Unlike acupuncture, herbal medicine diagnosis can be conducted electronically by inspecting the photos of your tongue and analysis of your symptoms through detailed questionnaires. For long distance patients this is quite ideal as you don't have to travel abroad. Some of the conditions that TCM works well for: gastrointestinal issues, nervous problems, insomnia, stress, urinary issues, reproductive problems, gynecology, autoimmunity and most chronic conditions.
